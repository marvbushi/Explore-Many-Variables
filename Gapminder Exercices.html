Plots show us that life expectancy at bitrh doubled from 49.3 to 72.6 years. The histograms shifted to the right between 1960 and 2016. The data from 1960 is more spread out and is somewhat bi-modal, hinting at well-separated groups of countries. The 2016 data is closer to unimodal, less spread out and negatively skewed (the left tail is longer than the right tail). In other words, the majority of countries are above the average an observation confirmed by the fact that median > mean.
